Mango Smoothie

Ingredients
  • 1 cup diced mango 150 g
  • ½ cup plain or vanilla Greek yogurt 120 g
  • ½ cup milk 120 mL

Vanila Smoothie

Ingredients
  • 8 full scoop butter pecan ice cream
  • 1 cup almond milk
  • 1/2-1 cup bourbon
  • Whipped cream for topping
  • Pecans for garnish

Chocolate Smoothie

Ingredients
  • 3/4 cup 2% milk
  • 1-1/2 cups chocolate ice cream
  • 1/4 cup creamy peanut butter

Strawberry Smoothie

Ingredients
  • 2 cups diced fresh strawberries (frozen can also be used)
  • 2 cups strawberry milk (16 ounces)
  • 2 cups strawberry ice cream (16 ounces)
  • optional toppings whipped cream, strawberry sauce, diced strawberries, sprinkles

Kiwi Smoothie

Ingredients
  • ½ cup Greek yogurt (or coconut milk for vegan, and omit the water)
  • ¼ cup water, plus more as needed
  • 2 kiwi

Blueberry Smoothie

Ingredients
  • 1 cup frozen blueberries
  • 1 tablespoon chia seeds
  • 1 cup unsweetened coconut milk
  • Filtered water as needed

Watermelon Smoothie

Ingredients
  • 1 cup watermelon frozen
  • ½ cup cucumber sliced
  • ½ lime juicedt
  • ¾ cup water

Orange Smoothie

Ingredients
  • 2 large peeled oranges
  • ½ tablespoon orange zest
  • ¼ cup Greek yogurt
  • ½ teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 tablespoon maple syrup or honey (optional)
  • 2 to 2 1/2 cups ice

Banana Smoothie

Ingredients

For the blackening seasoning blend (makes extra)

  • 1 cup almond milk
  • ½ cup Greek yogurt
  • 2 bananas frozen
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ½ teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1 tablespoons honey optional
  • 2 tablespoons vanilla protein powder

Mango Smoothie

Making Proceger
  1. Peel and dice the mango, then set on a plate and freeze for about 30 minutes to quickly chill it. (Alternatively, use fully frozen mango pieces).
  2. Blitz all ingredients in a blender until smooth.

Vanila Smoothie

Making Proceger
  1. Combine all of the ingredients, except cream and pecans, in a blender. Mix well and top with whipping cream and pecans.

Chocolate Smoothie

Making Proceger
  1. In a blender, combine the milk, ice cream, peanut butter and syrup; cover and process until smooth. If desired, garnish with whipped cream, peanut butter cups and additional chocolate syrup.

Strawberry Smoothie

Making Proceger
  1. Add strawberries, strawberry milk, and strawberry ice cream to a high powered blender (or large vessel if using a handheld blender).
  2. Blend to smooth, then pour into glasses.
  3. Garnish with your favorite toppings.

Kiwi Smoothie

Making Proceger

Peel and chop the kiwi. Add all ingredients to the blender, adding the liquids first and breaking the banana into pieces. Blend until smooth, adding a splash more water if necessary. Eat immediately or store up to 1 day refrigerated in a covered jar.

Blueberry Smoothie

Making Proceger
  1. Place all the ingredients into a blender in the order above, and blend until smooth.
  2. If your smoothie is a bit too thick, add small amounts of water until you get the desired consistency.

Waterwelon Smoothie

Making Proceger

Place all ingredients in a blender and blend until well combined.

Orange Smoothie

Making Proceger
  1. Zest about ½ orange. Add the peeled oranges, orange zest,Greek yogurt, vanilla, maple syrup or honey, and ice to a blender. Blend until smooth.
  2. Enjoy immediately or store up to 1 day refrigerated.

Banana Smoothie

Making Proceger
  1. Place the ingredients in the order listed in the container of a Vitamix blender (or another high-powered blender).
  2. Blend, starting on low speed and increasing to high, for 30-60 seconds or until mixture is smooth.
