Chocolate Donuts

Making Proceger
  1. Mix dry ingredients in bowl.Beat in the eggs,milk,vanilla and butter. Beat until well blended.
  2. Spray donut pan with cooking spray.Fill each donut cup about 3/4 full(use a ziploc with the corner snipped to do this neatly).Fills about 8-10 donuts.
  3. Bake donuts in 325 oven for about 11-14 minutes.Allow to cool slightly.Remove from pan.
  4. While cooking make chocolate glaze.In small saucepan on medium low heat.Mix together the cream,milk,butter,vanilla and corn syrup.When butter is completely melted,turn heat to low and add in the chocolate.
  5. Remove from heat and whisk in the powdered sugar,adding it slowly until desired consistency.
  6. Dip each donut into the glaze,allow to cool.(optional-sprinkles with toppings).Then allow chocolate to set(about 15 minutes).

Pancakes

Making Proceger
  1. Put 100 grams plain flour,2 large eggs,300 ml milk,1 Tbsp sunflower and vegetable oil and a pinch of salt into a bowl or large jug,then whisk to a smooth batter.
  2. Set aside for 30 mins to rest if you have time,or start cooking straight away.
  3. Set a medium frying pan over a medium heat and carefully wipe it with some oiled kitchen paper.
  4. When hot,put your pancakes for 1 min on each side until golden,keeping them warm in a low oven as you go.
  5. Then serve with butter,syrup or your favourite filling.....and enjoy.

Chocolate cookies

Making Proceger
  1. Line 2 baking sheets with parchment paper.
  2. Cream togather the dark brown sugar,granulated sugar and butter in a stand mixer fitted with the paddle attachment,scraping the sides once,until fluffy,3 to 4 minutes.Add the eggs,once at a time,mixing after each addition.Mix in the vanilla.
  3. Sift together the flour,cocoa powder,baking soda and salt,then add in batches to the mixer,mixing until just combineed after each addition.Gently stir in the regular sized chocolate candies,as well as the chocolate chips and dark chocolate chunks.
  4. Using a 1/4-cup measure,portion the dough onto the two parchment baking sheets.Place the mini chocolate candies into the top portion of the dough.And place in the Refrigrator for 30 minutes.
  5. When ready to bake,preheat the oven to 350 degrees F.
  6. Remove the dough from the fridge and bake until the cookie centers are just set,18 to 20 minutes.Allow the cookies to sit for 10 minutes before tranferring to a cooling rack to cool completely.Then serve on tray or have it.Enjoy!

Chicken Nuggets

Making Proceger
  1. Cut chicken in a small cubes.
  2. Place chicken pieces in a large bowl.
  3. Add onion,garlic paste,black pepper and salt in it.
  4. Mix well.
  5. Place this mixture in a chopper to mince it.
  6. Then put Refrigrator for 20 minutes.
  7. Now make small nugget shapes out of the mixture.
  8. Place flour and bread crumbs seperately in two shallow plates.
  9. In a small bowl beat eggs.
  10. Place nuggets in flour.
  11. Then dip in egg and then coat in bread crumbs.
  12. Heat oil in a deep pan over medium heat.
  13. Deep fry nuggets for 5-6 minutes or until golden brown.
  14. Chicken Nuggets are ready.

Bread Pizza

Making Proceger
  1. Preheat the oven at 180 degree celcius for 10 minutes.
  2. chop all the veggies use for toppings.Place them aside.Make sure they are not wet.
  3. Place the slices of bread in the oven and toast them just for 2-3 minutes.this is done just to firm them up so that the bread doesn't get soggy when we load them with pizza sauce and the veggies.
  4. Once the bread slices are toasted,brush them with some oil,spread the pizza sauce on each slice.
  5. Top each slice with shredded mozzarella cheese.
  6. Place all the veggies of your choice over the shredded cheese.
  7. Add olives and some jalapenos.Sprinkle some chili falkes and some pizza seasonings.
  8. Top with some more shredded cheese.
  9. Place the prepared bread slices on the baking tray and bake them for 10 minutes at 180 degree celcius or until the cheeses gets or bubbly and melts.Remove them from the oven and place them on the cooling rack so that the bread pizza don't turn soggy.Now serve them piping hot.And enjoy!
